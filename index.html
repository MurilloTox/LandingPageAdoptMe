About PetFinder

At PetFinder, we believe every pet deserves a safe, loving, and permanent home. Our mission is to connect rescued animals with caring families and make the adoption process simple, transparent, and rewarding.

We work alongside shelters, rescue organizations, and volunteers to help dogs and cats find their perfect match. By providing detailed pet profiles and an easy adoption process, we aim to give every animal a second chance at a happy life.
